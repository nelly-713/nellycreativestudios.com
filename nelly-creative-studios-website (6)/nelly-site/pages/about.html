About Nelly

Nelly Creative Studios

Based in
New York City

Nelly Creative Studios is focused on crafting high-end, fine and custom jewelry for individuals who appreciate exceptional craftsmanship and artistic vision — creating some of the world's most creative and stunning pieces using the newest and oldest jewelry techniques.

Our work is known for its elegance, beauty and sophistication, always using the most beautiful gems and materials combined with creativity. We specialize in Ancient Greek Gold Granulation, rare natural gemstones, and micro pavé setting to the highest standards.

"Beauty, jewelry, craft and art are siblings and we love bringing these together."

The Designer

Nelly
Mendoza-Mendoza

Nelly first started making jewelry and discovered her love for it as a first year undergraduate at Dartmouth College in New Hampshire. She later spent four years at Tiffany & Co. in the Innovation and Design Workshop in New York City and is a GIA Gemology Graduate.

She designs and creates work inspired by Greek Mythology, museum collections and artists, music, the natural world, stories, and vintage jewelry designs such as those from Fabergé and Ventura, among others.

Her favorite part of designing and making jewelry is being able to bring beauty into the everyday through Nelly Creative Studios.
