About the Studio

Who We Are

Nelly Creative Studios is based in New York City and focused on crafting high-end fine and custom jewelry for individuals who appreciate exceptional craftsmanship and artistic vision — using both the newest and oldest jewelry techniques.

Founded by Nelly Mendoza-Mendoza — GIA Gemology Graduate, Tiffany & Co. alumna, and Dartmouth Creative Fellow — every piece is designed and made in-house in New York City. Beauty, jewelry, craft and art are siblings, and we love bringing these together.
