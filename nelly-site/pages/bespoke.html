How It Works

The Creation Process

01

Consultation

A meeting of worlds where we discuss what is in your mind, what story you would like to tell with the piece, and what materials inspire you. Share inspiration images, sketches, or stories.

02

Sketch & Design

After the initial consult, a sketch is prepared followed by a hand gouache rendering — an old watercolor technique — bringing the design to life before the physical creation begins.

03

Stone Selection

If your design requires color stones or center stones, they are sourced from a wide range of suppliers. A 50% deposit is due once you are happy with the design intent.

04

Atelier Work

Savoir-faire turns the design into reality. Every curve is meticulously done, every stone is delicately set, every surface carefully finished. Then — the wait is over.
