Materials & Care

We work primarily with 22k gold, 18k gold, pink gold, platinum, and silver. All metals are sourced responsibly. The recommended metal for your piece will be discussed based on the design and your preferences.
Store each piece separately in a soft cloth pouch or the box provided. Keep away from perfumes, lotions, and harsh chemicals. For gold and platinum pieces, gentle cleaning with a soft brush and mild soap is recommended. For gemstone-set pieces, consult us for specific care instructions based on the stone type.
Yes. Nelly personally sources all gemstones and works with a curated network of trusted suppliers. Provenance and ethical sourcing are a priority. For rare or significant stones, documentation of origin is provided where available.
Ancient Greek Gold Granulation is a technique dating back over 4,000 years in which tiny spheres of gold (granules) are fused onto a gold surface without the use of solder — creating intricate textural patterns. Nelly studied this technique at Dartmouth College and received multiple awards for her mastery of it. It is one of the studio's signature specialties.
