Bespoke Jewelry

At Nelly Creative Studios, each bespoke creation begins with a conversation, a meeting of creativity and craft

Each detail is meticulously finished and each stone is selected by Nelly. Each piece is crafted in New York City

Design in progress; 22 karat gold cross

The creation process

01 — Consultation: a meeting of worlds where we discuss what is in your mind, what story you would like to say with the piece, what materials inspire you

Imagination is where it begins but we put it into the physical dimension

An initial consultation will consist of any inspiration images, sketches or stories you have. Before speaking we will review all of this information

There is no budget too small or too big; we work with small custom projects to the high custom design with rare stones and more complex designs requiring more time and resources

02 — Sketch and Design Delivery: After the initial consult, we are ready for a sketch and then once the design has been established a hand gouache rendering — an old technique with watercolor, bringing to life the design

The creation of a piece, the sketch phase — involves a delicate technique called gouache and is carefully created for custom pieces before the creation of a design in physical form. The blueprint for the workshop
Nelly Creative Studios ©

And a deposit of 50% the total cost of the project is due once you are happy with the design intent

03 — Stone Selection: If your design requires any color stones or center stones this is when the stones will be sourced and selected from a wide range of suppliers as required. If any review is needed on your end we will meet over video or in our studio

Natural Colombian cushion cut emeralds directly from the miners and dealer

04 — Atelier Work: Once all materials have been sourced and the idea is clear, savoir-faire will turn the design into a reality. Every curve is meticulously done, every stone is delicately set and every surface carefully finished

a piece in platinum in progress

05 — Creation: The wait is over — after many many hours of work

Custom set of earrings in 18k gold featuring Ruby, Sapphire, Emerald, Diamond and Aquamarine
Your creation awaits!
