ABOUT THE PIECE

One of the most technically demanding surfaces and techniques, gold granulation on a large curved surface, presented in an organic design. The piece contrasts with the perfection of the granules and the geometry to the seemingly random wavy and unified band.

All materials and parts are of the highest absolute quality possible, made from recycled metal, responsibly sourced and all parts are custom made and designed by Nelly in New York City.
